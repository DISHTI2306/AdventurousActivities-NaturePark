Animal Feeding at D&D Park!


Location East-West
Operating Days Monday to Saturday
Duration Flexible
Activity Time 10:00 - 17:00
Mininum Age 5
Transportation Offered as supplement

D&DPark offers a very friendly and secure environment for the children, youngsters and elders to play, interact and form wonderful memories with the animals. A day at D&DPark is certain to be full of action, fun, and excitement.
Animal feeding is a pleasant sense for everyone who enjoys discovering new experiences that they will remember for the rest of their lives. It is an excellent way for children, particularly young children, to learn about animals and become more acquainted with nature.

As part of the Animal Feeding you will enjoy:

  1. Tortoise Feeding
  2. Birds Feeding
  3. Giraffe Feeding
  4. Monkey Feeding

Safety Precautions:
  • Avoid hand-feeding the animals, as this can lead to injuries and diseases.
  • If you notice the animals acting strangely and aggressively, do contact the animal control professional immediately.
