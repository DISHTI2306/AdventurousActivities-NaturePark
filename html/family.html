Fun Family Adventure at D&d Park


D&D Park is described as a wonderful and enchanting area as well as an outstanding nature reserve in the south of Mauritius where all the nature lovers can enjoy themselves.

In our protected area, you may have a wonderful day out with your family by participating in our entertaining activities such as Mauritian Safari and Animal Feeding.

The family package at D&D Park is an invitation to get away from the crowded beaches and enjoy the natural beauty of D&D Park.

As part of this family package, you will get an energy boost by enjoying a good lunch at D&D restaurant, located in the east-west of the D&D Park.

The Family Package at D&D Park includes:
  • Animal Feeding
  • Colored Earth
  • Lunch - Italian Food + Homemade Juice
  • Onsite activities

Safety Requirements

Pregnant women and persons with physical disabilities are not permitted to participate in strenuous activities.

Additional Information

What to wear:

  • Sport shoes with socks (highly recommended)

What to bring:

  • Sunscreen
  • Mosquito repellant
