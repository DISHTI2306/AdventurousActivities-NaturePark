Welcome to D&DPark's Mauritian Safari


The advantage of traveling with us is that we take the guess work out of where to go and we are very concerned about all the little details to make your Mauritian Safari adventure worry free.

The safari takes place in the south district, in the region of Riviere-Des-Anguilles. Needless to say, the animal species viewing are unparalleled.

We will drive off the road, through the river, under the trees and over the trees. Because there has been no hunting at the preserve and only land rovers travel through the bushes, the animals do not see the vehicles as anything out of the ordinary in their natural environment. As a result, we can get extremely near to them without troubling them. This equalsÂ to fantastic viewing and photographic possibilities. Moreover, there is 10 km of river running through the 2 edges of the park, which ensures that the animal species will have constant source of water during the seasons.

If you are looking for the ultimate opportunity to see the amazing species that D&DPark has to offer, up close and in their natural habitat, then look no further.


Safety Precaution:

Safety during a safari is of extreme importance. A safari can be very exciting but coming in close contact with the animals can be dangerous if you do not follow the rules.

  • You should be careful of the animal's behavior and move away as soon as the animals start to appear aggitated.
  • If you encounter an animal that does not like your presence move back slowly and quietly.
