About Us
Our Story, Purpose and Goal

D&D Park is a natural site that is specialized in the provision of nature (outdoor) and adventurous activities through a well-designed safe park. We know the importance of doing these activities in a safe and secure environment. The park has a purpose to inspire people to form a bond with nature and to promoteeco-tourism in Maurtius. D&D Park has a goal to offer the best deals for locals as well as foreigners to enjoy the activities We always ensure important information such as activities and packages the park offers are well transmitted to all oiur visitors. We always check weather forecast before allowing people to do activities and we provide all the necessary protective equipments. We promote wildlife, fauna and flora protection awareness among people by organizing sensitization campaign regularly in the park. At D&D Park, we care for you and your families. This is why we pursue youngsters and elders to lead healthy lifestyle through educative bill boards in the park that displays the information of the imporatance of doing the activities the park proposes.
